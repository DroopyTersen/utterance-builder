Cheatsheet

Multiple Phrasing Options

This lets you define multiple ways to say a phrase, but combined into a single sample utterance

Template

{what is the|what's the|check the} status
Output

what is the status
what's the status
check the status

Optional Words

This lets you generate an utterance with and without the word

Template

what is your {favorite |} color
Output

what is your color
what is your favorite color

Custom Slot Types

To work with Custom Slot Types registered in your interaction model, use a special syntax to leave a curly-braced slot name unparsed.

Template

give me the stock price of {-|COMPANY}
Output

give me the stock price of {COMPANY}
